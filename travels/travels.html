The wind led me to push the doors of twelve countries where I had the pleasure of traveling, sharing with the locals and sometimes the chance to live. This is how I found myself spending 6 months in Guadeloupe, 3 months in Italy, 10 days in Ivory Coast doing humanitarian work and currently in Colombia where I have been packing since November 2021.

These trips taught me a lot and developed my ability to adapt in a team, to find solutions alone, faced with a situation in the midst of people who did not speak my language.

I share with you some places in photos before adding a “blog” category where we can even exchange together, I can’t wait 🤗
